A Future Friend

Welcome to our website!

We have been splurging so hard, often without stopping (!) and have got some amazing ideas about out characters in the future.

We hope you will enjoy how great our writing is!

Arda, Haringey Young Pirate

The visionary Young Pirates have travelled 1000 years into the future and sent us back this website to enjoy escaping into. They will introduce us to the exciting, funny or even scary characters of the future. Then through the eyes of these futuristic characters we will learn about what the future holds.

This project is the product of ten sessions of hard work with their volunteer crewmates, planning, splurging and refining to make sure their writing is the best they can be. They even worked with artists and web designers to input on the visuals to accompany it. We'd like to give a special shout-out to all our volunteer crewmates, designers and our web designer Andrew Hughes-Onslow

We hope you are thrilled and entertained by the stories you hear. See you somewhere in the future...

Lily Eastwood, Director of Learning, and the team at The Literacy Pirates!
